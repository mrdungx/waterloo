## Message to Hannah Choi

Hi Hannah! We're building something new at AhaSlides — a Lesson Publisher that lets trainers create self-paced lessons right inside the platform. Import a presentation or build from scratch, add text/video/quizzes, publish, and get a shareable link. Learners go through it at their own pace and you see their progress in a dashboard.

I put together an early wireframe — would love your honest feedback given how creatively you use AhaSlides in your coaching: https://mrdungx.github.io/waterloo/wireframe-lesson-publisher.html

Click the tabs at the top to see all 4 screens (editor, learner registration, learner view, trainer dashboard). On the dashboard, try switching between "Lessons" and "Learners" tabs.

Any thoughts welcome — what works, what's missing, what would make this useful for you?
